    What this audit can — and cannot — see

    This audit inspects your browser, device fingerprint, network exposure, and known account breaches. It does not — and technically cannot from a web page — scan the apps or background processes on your phone. For deep process/persistence scanning of a Mac, use the Mac Deep Scan below.

    Everything shown above is measured live on your device, in your browser, at the moment you run the audit. Nothing is stored or sent anywhere except the specific lookups each check describes.

    Mac Deep Scan

    The real "what is secretly running, auto-starting, or phoning home" audit — for your own Mac. Downloads a small local tool (no installation, nothing sent anywhere; the report stays on your Mac).

    Download Mac Deep Scan
    1. Download, then open the ZIP in Downloads — it unpacks a Privacy Deep Scan folder.
    2. First run only: right-click Privacy-Deep-Scan.command → Open → Open (macOS gatekeeps unknown downloads).
    3. A report opens automatically in your browser when the scan finishes (about a minute).

    Prefer to read the code first? The scanner is one plain, commented Python file: deep_scan.py.

    Add to your home screen

    On iPhone/iPad: tap the Share button in Safari, then Add to Home Screen. The audit then opens like an app and works offline.

    All checks are read-only and inspect only your own browser and device. Honesty over theatre: anything we can't measure says so.
